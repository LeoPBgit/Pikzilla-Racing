THE FORCE BEHIND PIKZILLA RACING

Pikzilla Racing isn't just a team—it's a family united by an unrelenting passion for automotive excellence. While we may compete as enthusiasts rather than professionals, our commitment to performance and precision is second to none.

Our diverse crew brings together driving talent, engineering brilliance, and operational expertise to create a formidable presence both on and off the track. With each member pushing the boundaries of what's possible, each member plays a crucial role in our collective success.

What truly sets the Pikzilla team apart isn't just skill or knowledge—it's heart. The camaraderie, mutual respect, and shared determination to excel create an environment where innovation thrives and where the love for the sport, the cars, and each other's company proves that this is more than a hobby—it's a genuine love for racing.

BECOME PART OF PIKZILLA RACING

Pikzilla Racing is always looking for passionate individuals to join our extended team. Whether you're a skilled mechanic, an aspiring driver, or simply an enthusiast with a desire to be part of something special, we'd love to hear from you.

While we're not a professional racing team, we enjoy an enthusiastic following and are building a community that shares our passion for motorsport. From track days to social events, and everything in between, there's a place for everyone in the Pikzilla family.

Welcome to the Pikzilla Racing blog—your gateway to the stories, insights, and adventures that fuel our passion for motorsport. Here, we share everything from technical deep-dives and race reports to the human stories that make our team special.

Whether you're a dedicated gear-head looking for performance insights, a casual enthusiast curious about supercar culture, or simply interested in following our journey, you'll find content that informs, entertains, and inspires. Our team members contribute their unique perspectives, sharing expertise and experiences from both on and off the track.

Dive in to discover what makes Pikzilla Racing more than just a team—it's a community united by a love for automotive excellence and the pursuit of speed.

HIGHLIGHTED STORIES

Founded in 2019 by a group of supercar enthusiasts with a shared dream, Pikzilla Racing has quickly evolved from weekend track days to becoming one of the most passionate amateur racing teams in the region. We don't just drive cars—we live and breathe the culture of automotive excellence.

Our philosophy is simple: combine cutting-edge performance engineering with raw driving talent and an unquenchable thirst for adrenaline. While we may not compete at the professional level, our dedication to the art and science of racing is second to none.

Join us on our journey as we push boundaries, break personal records, and share our love for the world's most extraordinary machines.

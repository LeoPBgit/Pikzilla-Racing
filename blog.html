Podium Finish at Monaco Grand Prix: A Weekend to Remember

The streets of Monte Carlo witnessed a masterclass in precision driving as Chris Gurko secured a spectacular second-place finish for Pikzilla Racing at the prestigious Monaco Grand Prix. In a race filled with drama, strategy, and skill, our team demonstrated why we're a force to be reckoned with in the world of motorsport.

The weekend began with promising practice sessions, where our engineers worked tirelessly to fine-tune the car's setup for the unique challenges of Monaco's tight and twisting circuit. Qualifying saw Chris secure a front-row start, setting the stage for Sunday's thrilling race.

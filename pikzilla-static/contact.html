Frequently Asked Questions

How can I sponsor Pikzilla Racing?

We offer various sponsorship packages tailored to different needs and budgets. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@pikzillaracing.com or fill out the contact form selecting "Sponsorship Opportunity" as the subject.

Are there job opportunities at Pikzilla Racing?

We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team. Check our careers page regularly for open positions, or send your resume to careers@pikzillaracing.com to be considered for future opportunities.

How can I purchase team merchandise?

Official Pikzilla Racing merchandise will be available soon through our online store. Follow us on social media for updates on merchandise releases.

How can I attend a race as a Pikzilla Racing guest?

We offer exclusive fan packages that include premium seating, pit lane walks, and meet-and-greet sessions with our drivers. Visit our Racing page for more information or contact our fan experience team at fans@pikzillaracing.com.

How can media representatives contact the team?

For media inquiries, interviews, or press kit requests, please contact our media relations team at media@pikzillaracing.com or fill out the contact form selecting "Media Request" as the subject.

Can I visit the Pikzilla Racing headquarters?

We occasionally organize open days for fans to visit our headquarters. These events are announced on our website and social media channels. For special visit requests, please contact us through the form above.

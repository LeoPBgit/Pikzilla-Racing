THE PEOPLE BEHIND THE SPEED

Meet the dedicated professionals who make Pikzilla Racing a force to be reckoned with on the track.

Our Team

At Pikzilla Racing, we believe that our greatest asset is our people. Our team brings together diverse talents and expertise, united by a shared passion for motorsport and a commitment to excellence. From our skilled drivers to our innovative engineers and dedicated support staff, every member plays a vital role in our success.

Micah Gurko

Team Principal

With over 20 years of experience in motorsport, Micah brings strategic vision and leadership to Pikzilla Racing. His innovative approach has transformed the team into a competitive force.

Chris Gurko

Lead Driver

A rising star in the racing world, Chris has already secured multiple podium finishes. His exceptional skill and determination make him a formidable competitor on any track.

Ezra Gurko

Technical Director

Ezra's engineering genius is behind many of our technical innovations. His deep understanding of vehicle dynamics and aerodynamics gives us a competitive edge.

Our Departments

Engineering

Our engineering team combines cutting-edge technology with innovative thinking to optimize vehicle performance. From aerodynamics to engine tuning, they leave no stone unturned in the pursuit of speed and reliability.

Operations

The operations team ensures that everything runs smoothly both on and off the track. From logistics to race strategy, their meticulous planning and execution are essential to our success at every event.

Support

Our support staff includes mechanics, technicians, and pit crew members who work tirelessly behind the scenes. Their expertise and dedication ensure that our vehicles and drivers are always ready to perform at their best.

Current Season: PZ-X23

The Pinnacle of Racing Technology

The PZ-X23 represents the culmination of our engineering team's expertise and innovation. Designed for the 2025 racing season, this vehicle combines aerodynamic efficiency, power, and handling precision to deliver exceptional performance on any track.

Our latest model features a redesigned chassis for improved weight distribution, advanced aerodynamic elements for enhanced downforce, and a state-of-the-art power unit that delivers both performance and reliability.

Key Features

Advanced Aerodynamics

Our aerodynamics package has been refined through extensive wind tunnel testing and computational fluid dynamics simulations. The result is a car that generates optimal downforce while minimizing drag, allowing for exceptional cornering speeds and stability.

Hybrid Power Unit

The PZ-X23 features our most advanced hybrid power unit to date. Combining a high-revving internal combustion engine with cutting-edge energy recovery systems, it delivers exceptional power and efficiency throughout the race.

Lightweight Chassis

Our carbon fiber monocoque chassis represents the perfect balance between strength and weight. Engineered to provide maximum driver protection while minimizing mass, it forms the foundation of the car's exceptional handling characteristics.

Adaptive Suspension

The PZ-X23's suspension system features advanced adaptive damping technology that continuously adjusts to track conditions and driving inputs. This provides optimal tire contact and grip in all situations, from high-speed straights to technical corners.

Advanced Electronics

Our proprietary electronic control systems manage everything from power delivery to differential settings. With thousands of parameters monitored in real-time, the car can be fine-tuned for optimal performance on any track and in any conditions.

Carbon Ceramic Brakes

The braking system features carbon ceramic discs and specially developed calipers for exceptional stopping power and fade resistance. Combined with our regenerative braking system, it provides both performance and efficiency.

Development Process

From Concept to Track

The development of each Pikzilla Racing car begins long before the season starts. Our design team works closely with engineers, aerodynamicists, and drivers to create a vehicle that pushes the boundaries of what's possible within the regulations.

The process begins with extensive computer modeling and simulation, followed by wind tunnel testing of scale models. As the design evolves, full-size components are manufactured and tested, culminating in the assembly of the complete car.

Before its competitive debut, each car undergoes rigorous testing on various tracks and in different conditions. This allows us to fine-tune performance and ensure reliability when it matters mostâ€”on race day.

Throughout the season, our development team continues to refine and improve the car, introducing updates based on data analysis and driver feedback. This relentless pursuit of performance is what drives Pikzilla Racing forward.

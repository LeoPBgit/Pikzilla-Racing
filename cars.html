CAR

Red Scorpion

McLaren 720S

The flagship of our fleet, this McLaren 720S has been transformed into a track weapon through extensive yet thoughtful modifications. With its distinctive Pikzilla Racing livery and aggressive aerodynamic package, The Scorpion announces its presence with authority.

The heart of this beast remains the twin-turbocharged 4.0L V8, now producing an astonishing 750 bhp thanks to custom ECU mapping and an enhanced cooling system. A fully adjustable racing suspension setup, lightweight forged wheels, and competition-grade carbon ceramic brakes complete the performance equation.

Inside, a careful balance maintains road usability while incorporating the safety and data acquisition systems essential for serious track use. The Scorpion represents the perfect balance of technological sophistication and raw, visceral performance.
